Undusted Texts

Brandon P. Otto
Author and Translator

This website serves as a hub for my work. My blog includes shorter writings and translations (for the latter, see the blog's translation index). Links to my longer essays, articles, and translations can be found on the publications page; most can be downloaded in PDF form from the links provided there, or from my Academia.edu page. To purchase my self-published books of translations, follow the link to the Undusted Texts Translations page on Amazon; more details about each book can be found on the individual product pages or at my blog.

Latest Updates

I am proud to announce my first traditionally-published book, My Burden Is Light: Suffering and Consolation in the Christian Life by St. John of Ávila (1499-1569), published by TAN Books. It is available in both hardcover and ebook formats. To promote the book, I have had some recent interviews, links to which can be found here.

My most recent long-form self-publication is a selection of writings on St. Joan of Arc by Maurice Barrès (1862-1923), entitled Joan of Arc: Hero of Lorraine, Hero of France, Hero of Christendom.

As for my shorter, eBook-only translations, I have recently published another selection of short writings by Pierre de Bérulle, On the Passion of Christ.
